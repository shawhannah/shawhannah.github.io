About Me


I'm Hannah and I live in Raleigh, NC. I graduated from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill in 2016. I earned a Bachelor of Arts in public relations from the School of Media and Journalism. In the years following graduation, I worked in digital marketing, communications, and technology. Currently, I work at IBM as an Offering Manager for IBM Z and I'm earning a certification in web development from UNC-Chapel Hill.


When I'm not in class or at work, I'm usually with friends at a local brewery or reading a book. Being relatively new to Raleigh, I still have a lot of places to explore. I live with my cat Luna near PNC Arena. Neither of us are big fans of hockey or the traffic that comes with it. When we're home, Luna prefers lying in the sunlight while I prefer watching Netflix or online shopping.
